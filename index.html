About

Hi! I'm Tyler Parker (he/him).

These days, I wear a lot of hats, but my favorites are those of communications specialist, media producer, and video editor. I hold a B.A. in Communications and Media Studies from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, and I graduated in August 2022. During my time at UNC, I studied mass communications, documentary and narrative filmmaking, visual effects, graphic design, web development, and data science. After graduation, I moved back to Raleigh and continued to sharpen my creative skills with gigs in live production. I also took on contract work as a CAD designer and datacenter operations specialist, but I'm still a storyteller at heart. I'm hoping to find a professional home where I can help tell compelling stories, be it in communications, public relations, or media production. Outside of work, you can find me buried in a comic book, cheering on my favorite Formula 1 teams, tweaking my custom-built racing simulator, or writing/playing music in my home studio.
